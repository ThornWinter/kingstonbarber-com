Mostafa Oukili is an internationally recognized, award winning Master Barber offering high quality barber services at very reasonable prices.

Mostafa apprenticed under his brother in Morocco, a country well known for the quality of their barber services.

"I cut hair because I love it," Mostafa says, "it is in my blood."

Mostafa was featured on the Montel Williams Show in 1994 and won First Place in the 1986 Maghrib Arab, an International Barber's Competition requiring Mostafa to qualify in his home country of Morocco and then defeat other hair cutting champions from around the world.

Mostafa was a professional barber at Aster Place Barbershop in Manhattan for over twenty years. He now brings his talent, creativity, and passion for grooming to the city of Kingston.
